About Me

I’m a Computer Science student with a strong focus on backend engineering, where logic, architecture, and performance come together to build meaningful systems. My journey into software development began with a deep curiosity about how things work behind the curtain, from designing scalable APIs to optimizing data flow and server logic.

As my technical foundation has matured, so has my ambition. I'm now venturing into frontend development to better unify backend intelligence with intuitive, expressive user interfaces. I believe that great software isn’t just functional it should feel effortless, engaging, and human.

Over the past few years, I’ve worked on projects that challenged me to think critically about performance, security, and user experience refining not just my technical skills, but also my creative problem-solving abilities.

Outside of coding, you’ll find me exploring interface design trends, experimenting with new tools, or contributing to open-source projects that push the web forward. I thrive on learning, building, and turning complex ideas into elegant digital solutions that leave a lasting impact.
